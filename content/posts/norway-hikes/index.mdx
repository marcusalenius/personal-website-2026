## Day 3: Trolltunga Hike

The road between P1 Tyssedal and P2 Skjeggedal was closed overnight for rockfall protection work. We drove to P2 a bit after it opened at 6am, where we parked. It is possible to start the hike at P2 — in which case it would be a 27 km (16.8 mile) round trip with an 800 m (2,600 ft) ascent — however, between P2 and P3 you'd walk along a switchback road where cars (the ones who were lucky enough to get one of the few parking spots at P3) and shuttle busses drive. Instead of doing this we opted to take the shuttle bus to P3, which cost about $15. Well worth it to start the actual trail fresh and early enough to be ahead of a lof of the crowds.

From P3 it's a 20 km (12.4 mile) round trip with a 320 m (1,000 ft) ascent. It is advertised to take 7–10 hours round trip. We did the hike to Trolltunga in 3 hours. There is one large ascent in the beginning. The rest is a lot of up, down, and flat, traversing the mountain tops.
